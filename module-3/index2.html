In Day 1, we learn about Laser cutting,Laser cutting is a manufacturing process that uses a high-powered laser beam to cut through materials such as metal, wood, plastic, glass, and various other materials. The laser beam is typically generated by a laser resonator and focused through a series of mirrors or lenses onto the material being cut.
